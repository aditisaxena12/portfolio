About me

I'm Aditi and I'm a

Essentially, I'm a third year undergraduate student pursuing Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Goa. But I like to describe myself as an "old-school techie". As much as I feel fascinated by the fast-paced advances in the digital world, I always feel at-home with those melodious retro songs and yellow paged classics. Coding gives me an adrenaline rush but literature soothes my soul. (Coding will earn me money and literature will make me broke xD).

Having explored different technical domains like robotics, app development, cyber security and web development, I have finally found my passion in Artificial Intelligence. Of particular interest, are Computer Vision models.

If not in front of the computer screen, you'll find me engrossed in a thrilling fiction or strumming a guitar to my favorite songs or even struggling to get the right blend of watercolors. I believe in making the most out of each moment and doing what I love. This is me and I'm glad to meet you.
